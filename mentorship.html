Mentorship

Our mentorship program aims to to connect students with a mentor within our organziation. Members can sign up to be both a mentee or a mentor. We a re looking to develop organic mentor-mentee relationships and provide a space for students to talk about any concerns or challenges they may face as first-generation college students.
